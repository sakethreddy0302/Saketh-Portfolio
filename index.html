Virtual Voice Assistant (Jenny)

Jan 2023 - Jan 2024

AI has revolutionized virtual assistants like Siri and Alexa, but Jenny, a new virtual voice assistant for Windows. Designed with accessibility in mind, Jenny aids handicapped users, especially those unable to type or see. Using Python and NLP, Jenny understands and responds to voice commands contextually, allowing users to open apps, browse the internet, and control media. This provides users with greater independence and demonstrates AI's significant potential for inclusive technology. Jenny's development is a major step toward enhancing accessibility for people with disabilities.
